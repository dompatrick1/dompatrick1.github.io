Live Link

Etzie is a clone of Etsy but with only outdoor gear, my first solo project. Split into four categories: fishing, hunting, camping, and hiking. Users can search and browse these categories, add items to their cart and leave product reviews.

Live Link

console.game - goodreads clone

Live Link

Console.game is a group project I contributed to that is a clone of goodreads but for video games. Users can search games, leave reviews and ratings, and add them to a shelf to keep track of what they have played, want to play, or are currently playing.
